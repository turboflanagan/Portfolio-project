About me

Who is Peter Flanagan?

Peter is a web developer with a love of building awesome stuff. Getting his start at DigitalCrafts, a 16 week immersive bootcamp, Peter has enjoyed learning 2 full web stacks (MEAN and LAMP), as well as Swift and building native iOS apps. Naturally gifted with an analytical personality, figuring out how things work has always been an exciting endeavor.

As a kid, he got started early with Lego sets and always loved how versatile and useful the toy bricks were. As Peter grew up, his love of learning how stuff works and enjoyment of building things was applied to a broad range of things. From building race cars to building and training teams of people, Peter has learned to apply his analytical mind and love of building things to projects and problems to make some really awesome stuff.
